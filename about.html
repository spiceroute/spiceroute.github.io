About Us


Spice Route Bakery is a home-based Boutique Bakery specializing in custom cakes and cupcakes. In addition to a wide range of cake and filling flavors, we offer our signature "Indian dessert" inspired treats like our Gulab Jamun cupcakes, Gajar Halwa cupcakes, Mango and Shrikhand cupcakes, Rabri-Jalebi cupcakes, Kulfi cheesecake etc. All the signature flavors are made without any eggs in them!

We cherish the sentiments behind each of our celebration cakes and work with you to design a cake uniquely suited to your taste and personality.



Taste and Quality

The taste of your cake is the most important detail! Spice Route Bakery uses only top-quality ingredients to create delicious and unique cakes. Our signature line of "Indian Desserts" inspired cakes and cupcakes are made with fresh, fragrant and luxurious Indian spices such as cardamom, saffron, etc. The gulab jamuns used in the Gulab Jamun Cupcakes are made fresh and so are the jalebis for the Rabri-Jalebi Cupcakes.

Our indulgent Vanilla Buttercream is made with high quality butter & finished with European white chocolate for a luxurious texture. All fruit buttercreams are made with real fruit purées.



Design and Decoration

Spice Route Bakery wants your cake to reflect your unique style and personality. We hold detailed consultations including tastings, upon request, to make your cake the highlight of your celebration. We have several designs to spark your imagination and we welcome photos and pictures from magazines. We can also custom design your cake based upon the theme of your event.
