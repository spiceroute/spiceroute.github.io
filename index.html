Our Story

Spice Route Bakery is a home-based Boutique Bakery specializing in custom cakes and cupcakes. In addition to a wide range of cake and filling flavors, we offer our signature "Indian dessert" inspired treats like our Gulab Jamun cupcakes, Gajar Halwa cupcakes, Mango and Shrikhand cupcakes, Rabri-Jalebi cupcakes, Kulfi cheesecake etc. All the signature flavors are made without any eggs in them! We cherish the sentiments behind each of our celebration cakes and work with you to design a cake uniquely suited to your taste and personality.
