How much cake?

Spice Route Bakery believes that the celebration cake should shine! For that reason, we recommend serving our cakes as the main dessert. A two tier cake (9" and 6") would serve a party of 50 people while a 3 tier cake (12", 9" and 6") would serve a party of 70 people.
