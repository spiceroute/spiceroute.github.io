Reviews

It was my little one's first birthday, and we had planned for a small celebration with close friends. When I found out about Archana's business, I immediately knew that this was how I could give the birthday party a very personal touch. Since I didn't want a store bought generic cake, I asked Archana for options. She worked with me to figure out what were Amia's favorite things and what I wanted the cake to be. I can only say that the cake brought the party together. It was a layered vanilla with strawberry cheesecake frosting, with flowers, butterflies and strawberries in an elegant bouquet on fondant frosting. It was delicious, and pretty! Thanks a ton for making our special day memorable!!~Manasi Shukla-Kher
